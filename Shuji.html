PELAU Alliance Requirements

Alliance Regulations & Requirements

  • Your corporation must be actively developed or in the process of development. We do not impose a specific membership requirement, but active engagement is essential.
  • Your corporation must demonstrate professionalism and have no negative history within the ROBLOX community.
  • Your corporation must appoint one to two representatives from each relevant department to join our communications server. These representatives should be available for ongoing communication.
  • Your corporation is required to maintain a Discord server for clear communication and work-related discussions.
  • Your corporation must be entirely original and not a copy of existing games.

If your corporation is accepted into an alliance with Pelau, the following expectations must be met:

  • Your corporation must maintain a professional and respectful demeanor within our premises and adhere to our regulations.
  • Your corporation is expected to announce any applications or events hosted, if requested by us.

Alliance application

  • Inform us on the name of your corporation. Provide ROBLOX group/Discord links.
  • Inform us of your group, we would like to know more about who is interested to form an alliance with us.
  • Provide us one- to two representatives who will be joining our premises and whom we can communicate with.

Please note that you must send your alliance application in a separate Google document.
